Social Media Apps - and their features

  • News Feed
  • Friends and Followers
  • Photos and Videos
  • Live Streaming
  • Messaging(Messenger)

  • Feed(Posts and Stories)
  • IGTV(long-form videos)

  • Tweets(short messages)
  • @mentions
  • Trends
  • Followers and Following
  • Live Streaming

  • Profiles and Connections
  • Job Search and Recruitment
  • News and Publishing
  • Learning(online courses)

  • Video sharing and streaming
  • Channels and Subscriptions
  • Comments and Community
  • Monetization(ads and sponsorships)
  • Playlists and Recommendations
