Academics

Specialized Courses

Computer Networks
Covers the principles of networking, including protocols, network architecture, and data transmission methods. Topics may include TCP/IP, LANs, and WANs.
Database Management Systems (DBMS)
This course focuses on the design, implementation, and management of databases. It covers SQL, normalization, transactions, and database management systems.
Control Systems
Explores the modeling, analysis, and design of control systems for dynamic processes. It includes topics like feedback control, stability analysis, and control strategies.
Machine Design
Covers the principles of designing mechanical components and systems. Topics include stress analysis, material selection, and failure theories.

Electives and Advanced Courses

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
Focuses on algorithms and techniques for artificial intelligence and machine learning. Topics include supervised learning, neural networks, and natural language processing.
Robotics
Covers the design and control of robots, including kinematics, dynamics, and robotic sensors and actuators.
Renewable Energy Systems
Explores various renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydro power. It includes system design and integration issues.
Cybersecurity
Covers principles and practices for protecting computer systems and networks from security threats and attacks. Topics include cryptography, network security, and ethical hacking.
Internet of Things (IoT)
Focuses on the design and implementation of IoT systems, including sensors, communication protocols, and data processing.
