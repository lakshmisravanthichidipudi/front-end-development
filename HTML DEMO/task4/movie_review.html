Movie Review: "KALKI 2898AD"

Synopsis

KALKI is a modern-day avatar of Vishnu, a Hindu god, who is believed to have descended to earth to protect the world from evil forces.

Review

"Kalki 2898" is not just a movie; it's an experience that redefines the boundaries of science fiction and epic storytelling.
